# pizza-sales-dashboard-
📊 Interactive Power BI Pizza Sales Dashboard featuring sales analysis, revenue insights, KPI tracking, and data visualization for business decision-making.
🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard provides a detailed analysis of pizza sales data and helps identify key business trends. The dashboard is designed to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

Key Insights:
📈 Total Revenue generated from pizza sales.
🛒 Total Orders and Total Pizzas Sold.
💰 Average Order Value and Average Pizzas per Order.
🏆 Top-Selling and Bottom-Selling Pizza Categories.
📅 Daily and Monthly Sales Performance Analysis.
⏰ Peak Order Times and Customer Buying Patterns.
📊 Category-wise and Size-wise Sales Distribution.
🎯 Interactive filters for deeper business analysis.
Business Impact:

The dashboard helps business owners understand customer preferences, monitor sales performance, identify profitable products, and make data-driven decisions to improve revenue and operational efficiency.
